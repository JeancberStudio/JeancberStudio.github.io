Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

JeancberStudio ("we", "us", or "our") operates mobile games and related services (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is collected, used, and shared when you use our Service.

Types of Data Collected and Services Used

Advertising

AdMob

Personal Data Collected: Trackers; unique device identifiers for advertising (such as Google Advertiser ID or IDFA); usage data.

Other advertising partners may include:

  • AppLovin
  • ironSource
  • Pangle
  • Unity Ads
  • Chartboost

These services may collect usage data, advertising identifiers, and related information to provide and measure advertisements.

Analytics

Google Analytics for Firebase

Personal Data Collected: App launches and updates, device information, geography/region, in-app purchases, session count and duration, operating systems, trackers, and unique device identifiers.

Unity Analytics

Personal Data Collected: Usage data and other data as specified in Unity’s privacy policy.

A/B Testing

Firebase Remote Config

Personal Data Collected: Various data as specified in its privacy policy, used to test and optimize gameplay experiences.

Infrastructure Monitoring

Crashlytics

Personal Data Collected: Crash data, device information, and Universally Unique Identifiers (UUID).

Platform Services

Google Play Store

Personal Data Collected: Usage data.

App Store Connect

Personal Data Collected: Advertising data, diagnostics, product interaction data, trackers, unique device identifiers, and UUID.

Children’s Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal data from users under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal data from a child under 13 has been collected, we will take steps to delete such information as quickly as possible.

Interest-Based Advertising

We may use advertising partners such as AdMob to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

For Children:

Users can manage advertising preferences through their device settings, such as “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS or “Opt out of Ads Personalization” on Android. Users may also use tools such as AppChoices to manage interest-based advertising.
